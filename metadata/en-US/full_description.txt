Fetchify is a powerful screenshots manager that helps you organize, categorize, and manage your device screenshots with ease and efficiency.

Key Features:

- On-device AI for fully private processing
- Optional cloud models for faster performance
- Smart screenshot organization & categorization
- Advanced search and filtering
- Automatic duplicate detection & removal
- Batch operations for bulk management
- Material You theming and design
- Privacy-focused local storage
- Easy export and sharing
- Background monitoring for new screenshots
- Custom collections & tagging system
- Rich notifications and reminders
- OCR text recognition for selectable text in screenshots
- Supports 11 UI languages and more output languages

Perfect for heavy screenshot users who need a cleaner, more organized gallery.
No ads, completely free, and open source.
